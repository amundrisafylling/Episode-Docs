_Available from version 9.0.0.0_

You can easily copy individual cell values from the visual directly to your clipboard for quick use in other applications, such as Excel, Notepad, or an email.

To copy a cell value, perform the following steps:
1. Click on the cell containing the value you want to copy to select it.
2. Press Ctrl+C on your keyboard.

The value of the cell is now copied to your clipboard and is ready to be pasted. Currently, it is only possible to copy a single cell value.
