In Power Bi desktop you can get this pop-up window to see information about your visual.

<img width="421" alt="image" src="https://github.com/Profitbase/PowerBI-visuals-FinancialReportingMatrix/assets/148453680/6fac05dd-4c29-48bb-a7ed-c77776308a3f">

**Traditional Layout**
You can access this window by right-clicking the visual you want to check in the **build a visual** pane, and clicking "about"

<img width="289" alt="image" src="https://github.com/Profitbase/PowerBI-visuals-FinancialReportingMatrix/assets/148453680/7b5492a0-718a-4bba-a3b9-10240abe0552">


**On-object Interaction**

If you have selected the "on-object interaction" in the **preview feature** section, then you have to right-click the visual and click "about" in **build a visual** pane, rather than the insert visual section in the home ribbon.
<img width="587" alt="image" src="https://github.com/Profitbase/PowerBI-visuals-FinancialReportingMatrix/assets/148453680/0b234ae2-b6f3-49c2-80bf-c30fe1074b7c">
