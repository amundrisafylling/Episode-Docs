# CFO Platform

The Profitbase CFO Platform is a suite of integrated services and solutions built on the Profitbase Application Platform and Microsoft Fabric. It helps businesses efficiently plan, execute, and analyze to make better decisions faster.

![CFO Platform overview](/images/cfo-platform/CFO-Platform-Overview.svg)

## Business process automation

Profitbase offers a powerful business process automation platform for connecting systems, automating repetitive tasks, reducing errors, and freeing teams to focus on value-adding activities. Whether tracking inventory, managing budgets, or generating reports, automation brings precision and efficiency to your processes.

[Read more about business process automation](../cfo-platform/business-process-automation/overview.md)

## Data platform

The Profitbase Data Platform is built around [Microsoft Fabric](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/fabric/), making it easy to integrate with third-party data platforms, APIs, Power BI, and other analytics tools.

[Read more about the data platform](../cfo-platform/data-platform/overview.md)
